The Best Movies According to Ryan

My top 3 movies of all time.

Top Gun: Maverick

This is the epitomne of action movies. The sheer thrill you get from watching this masterfully produced film will invoke a sense of patriotism.

Titanic

The all-time classic. Although the ending was sad, the plot was amazing.

Mission Impossible Series

Fast-paced, full of surprises, the Mission Impossible series never dissapoints.
